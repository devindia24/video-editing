Our Story

Built by Creators, for Creators

MotionCraft Academy was founded in 2019 by a team of professional video editors and motion graphic artists who saw a gap in accessible, high-quality creative education.

Our mission is simple: give every aspiring creator the skills, tools, and community they need to turn their passion into a profession. We believe world-class creative education should be accessible to everyone.

Today, we're proud to have helped over 10,000 students land jobs at top studios, build successful freelance careers, and bring their creative visions to life.
